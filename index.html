Our Story

Crafted With Intention

KACHY DHAGGY was founded on a single belief: that clothing should be an heirloom, not a trend. Every collection begins in our atelier, where master tailors and textile artisans work in close collaboration to translate ideas into garments that endure.

We source fabrics from mills we trust, cut every pattern with precision, and finish each piece by hand. The result is fashion that feels less like a purchase and more like a promise — elegance that's built to last.
